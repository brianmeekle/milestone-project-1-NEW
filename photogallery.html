Get an insight into the wonderful world of Contraband Circus

through our collection of photos, art and flyers of past events.


Born and bred in Bristol in the UK, Contraband Circus

first arose in 2015 at a venue known as The Crofters Rights.

The first event brought Gypsy, Ska and Balkan bands to the table

with a side of ground based circus performance.

9 years later, Contraband is a strong and reputable name

in the South West of England and hosts many roadblock events.

Along the years, mutiple venues were danced in and many additions<

were made in the form of DJ's bringing electronic genres such as

Jungle, Tekno, Breakcore and more to the masses!


Check out our events page for future happenings from the Contraband Circus family

and their sister events such as Odd Top, Sporadic & Donk Stop Believing!!!
