Contraband Circus ...


"A veritable smorgasbord of sideshow performances and musical acts bound together with a twisted circus theme."


Born and bred in Bristol in the UK, Contraband Circus first arose in 2015 at a venue known as The Crofters Rights. The first event brought Gypsy, Ska and Balkan bands to the table with a side of ground based circus performance.


9 years later, Contraband Circus is a strong and reputable name in the South West of England, and hosts many roadblock events in venues such as The Loko Club, The Jam Jar & Cosies. Along the years, mutiple venues were danced in and many additions were made in the form of DJ's bringing electronic genres, such as Jungle, Tekno, Breakcore and more, to the masses.


Check out the events page for future happenings from the Contraband Circus family and also and other 'avin it events from their sister events such as Odd Top, Sporadic & Donk Stop Believing...


Keep those eyes open, ears to the ground and be prepared to get those knees up!!!
